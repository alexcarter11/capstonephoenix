Our Window Replacement Services

Windows do more than just let in light—they can save you money, keep your home cool, and boost its style. At Capstone Phoenix, we offer six types of windows to fit any home. Each type is designed to handle Arizona’s heat while making your home look amazing. Here’s what we offer:

Sliding Windows

These windows slide open easily and are great for big views. They’re perfect for living rooms or bedrooms where you want lots of light and airflow. Our sliding windows come with double-pane glass to keep heat out.

Double Hung Windows

Classic and versatile, these windows have two parts that slide up and down. They’re easy to clean and great for air circulation, especially in hot Phoenix summers. A Mesa family told us their new double-hung windows made their home feel fresher!

Awning Windows

These windows open from the top, letting you keep them open even during rain. They’re perfect for bathrooms or kitchens where you need ventilation without worrying about water. We installed awning windows for a Chandler home, and they loved the extra breeze.

Casement Windows

Casement windows swing open like a door, giving you maximum airflow. They’re great for kitchens or bedrooms where you want fresh air. Our uPVC casement windows are low-maintenance and energy-efficient, perfect for Glendale homes.

Picture Windows

Fixed windows that don’t open but offer huge, clear views. They’re perfect for showing off Arizona’s desert scenery. A Fountain Hills client chose our energy-efficient picture windows and said their living room feels brighter and cooler.

Specialty Windows

Want something unique? Our specialty windows come in shapes like arches or circles to match your home’s style. We helped a Paradise Valley homeowner add arched windows, and it gave their home a one-of-a-kind look.

Each window type is built to save energy and last for years. For example, our double-pane windows block out heat, which can cut your cooling costs by up to 20%. We also offer custom colors and sizes to match your home’s look, whether it’s modern or traditional.

French Doors

Specialty Doors

Our doors are built to handle Arizona’s heat and dust while keeping your home secure. For example, our sliding glass doors have strong locks and energy-efficient glass to keep your home safe and cool. We also offer screen door options for extra airflow without bugs.
